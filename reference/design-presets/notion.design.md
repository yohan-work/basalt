# Notion Inspired DESIGN.md

Source note:
- Inspired by the public Notion entry from VoltAgent `awesome-design-md`.
- Public summary used as seed: warm minimalism, serif-leaning editorial hierarchy, soft surfaces.
- This is a local Basalt preset for prompt steering, not an official Notion design system.

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
- Use a quiet, warm, approachable minimalism.
- The page should feel thoughtful, readable, and gently editorial.
- Favor calm structure over hard contrast or glossy marketing intensity.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- Prefer off-white, paper-like, or warm neutral backgrounds.
- Use charcoal or dark brown-gray text instead of stark pure black when the project allows it.
- Secondary surfaces should be soft and understated.
- Accent color should be sparse and muted.

## 3. Typography Rules
- Use clean sans body text with an editorial headline rhythm.
- Headings may feel more literary or document-like than product-aggressive.
- Body text should read naturally in longer passages.
- Favor measured hierarchy and readability over dramatic scale jumps.

## 4. Component Stylings
- Buttons should feel simple and unintrusive.
- Cards and content blocks should feel like gentle containers, not shiny product panels.
- Inputs should be quiet, soft-edged, and legible.
- Navigation should support reading and structure, not dominate the page.

## 5. Layout Principles
- Favor document-like flow and clear content grouping.
- Use generous but softer spacing than a luxury marketing page.
- Sections should feel organized and readable before they feel “designed.”
- This preset works well for docs, guides, productivity tools, and calm landing pages.

## 6. Depth & Elevation
- Use very light depth and soft tonal separation.
- Borders may be faint and friendly.
- Avoid high-gloss shadows or dramatic layered panels.

## 7. Motion
- Motion should be almost invisible.
- Prefer subtle fades and gentle reveals only where needed.
- Avoid theatrical transitions or aggressive parallax.

## 8. Do
- Make the page feel organized, warm, and readable.
- Let content structure lead the design.
- Use soft surfaces and quiet hierarchy.

## 9. Don't
- Do not convert this into a stark developer-dark interface.
- Do not use luxury-cinematic hero treatments.
- Do not introduce saturated color systems or loud marketing motion.
