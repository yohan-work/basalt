# Vercel Inspired DESIGN.md

Source note:
- Inspired by the public Vercel entry from VoltAgent `awesome-design-md`.
- Public summary used as seed: black and white precision, Geist-like clarity, developer-product minimalism.
- This is a local Basalt preset for prompt steering, not an official Vercel design system.

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
- Use a precise, technical, high-confidence visual language.
- Favor crisp monochrome composition over expressive decoration.
- The interface should feel fast, modern, and engineered rather than editorial or playful.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- Base the page on black, white, and neutral gray.
- Use accent color sparingly and only when it clarifies hierarchy.
- Prefer hard contrast, clean separators, and minimal chroma.
- Avoid warm palettes, luxury gloss, or candy-like gradients.

## 3. Typography Rules
- Prefer a modern grotesk or system sans with strong readability.
- Headlines should feel sharp and controlled, not oversized for drama.
- Body copy should be concise and technical.
- Reduce ornamental prose; this style benefits from compact, product-focused messaging.

## 4. Component Stylings
- Buttons should be compact, crisp, and direct.
- Cards, panels, and code-like surfaces should use clear boundaries and careful spacing.
- Inputs should feel product-grade and developer-tool friendly.
- Navigation should be spare, precise, and uncluttered.

## 5. Layout Principles
- Prefer contained layouts with disciplined spacing.
- Use strong alignment and clear structural rhythm.
- Hero sections can be bold, but they should stay restrained in color and copy length.
- Dense sections are acceptable when the hierarchy remains clean and legible.

## 6. Depth & Elevation
- Use subtle elevation and clean border contrast.
- Shadows should be restrained and secondary to tonal separation.
- Avoid soft dreamy layering or heavy glass effects.

## 7. Motion
- Motion should feel fast, subtle, and product-oriented.
- Prefer short fades, slight transforms, and purposeful transitions.
- Avoid cinematic drift, bounce, or decorative looping animation.

## 8. Do
- Keep UI sharp, minimal, and technically polished.
- Let black-and-white contrast carry the page.
- Present product messaging with confidence and brevity.

## 9. Don't
- Do not turn this preset into luxury editorial design.
- Do not introduce warm beige surfaces or handwritten softness.
- Do not overuse gradients, oversized pill UI, or playful visual gimmicks.
